Bentley A Great Place to Live, Work and Play!

Beautiful town among the rolling farmland

The thriving community of Bentley can be found in the beautiful blindman River Valley region of cenral Alberta. Nestled among the rolling farmland the town of Bentley sits between two of Alberta's prime lakes for families to enjoy. Bentley is what small towns are all about, getting together with co-workers friends and family with a vibrant town spirit.
